# Incident Response Procedures

## Symptom

An alert opens an incident or a human reports production impact.

## Diagnosis

Identify severity, affected services, workspaces, region, first bad timestamp, and deduplication fingerprint. Link dashboards, logs, traces, and GID evidence.

## Remediation

Assign commander and scribe, choose the relevant runbook, mitigate impact, update status, and keep all external alert references synchronized.

## Verification

Confirm user impact is resolved, close the incident, generate the post-mortem when required, and attach the final timeline.
