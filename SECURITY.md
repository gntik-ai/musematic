# Security Policy

Report suspected vulnerabilities to `security@musematic.ai`.

Please include the affected version, deployment mode, reproduction steps, expected
impact, and any relevant logs with secrets removed. Do not publish vulnerability
details publicly until the maintainers have acknowledged and triaged the report.

Musematic targets an initial acknowledgement within 24 business hours and a
remediation plan within 5 business days for confirmed issues.

The production PGP key and `security.txt` publication are operator-owned tasks.
Until they are published, use the email alias above and avoid sending secret
material in the initial report.

The full security guide lives in the documentation site under
`docs/security/responsible-disclosure.md`.
